You don't know

Much until you try,

Does the snow

Right come from the sky?

 \
Christmas tree,

Cinnamon and pine;

Are you free?

All this time is fine.

 \
Ho ho ho

The sadness' defeat

From a slow

Now speed up the beat!

 \
Love, enjoy

Whirling to the max,

Pure joy

Really *laeta pax*!
