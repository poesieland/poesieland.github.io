Give the sky a try

To blossom again,

Is this your pain

Or sorrow ?

Sit down and hope

For tomorrow.

But it's raining, nope.

 \
Give the sky a try,

It's Fall before Summer,

Yet just a bummer.

Even more days, so many

Will shine

Like a balm,

Crispy.

This will be fine.
