The wind has suffered

From loneliness

It came to my ear and breathes.

Suddenly I saw

A new landscape,

I heard

A new song,

Your song,

The song of love and friendship,

The universal Justice,

Life and death embodied.

The wind, from your lips

Begins a new world.
