You chose my name

But I'm not your thing

And you're not my king,

It's not the same.

 \
You gave me shelter,

With you no loneliness,

Always same tenderness,

I'm getting elder.

 \
Coz I am your pet,

Swear on your soul,

We're both, we're foul,

Coz you're just mine yet.
