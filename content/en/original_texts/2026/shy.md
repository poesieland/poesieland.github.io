Evergreen...

Hear the scream

From the screen

Of your dream.

 \
By design

Love is wide,

With a sign

You can't hide.

 \
No distress

Even they're

Your mistress

Over there.

 \
Don't be shy,

No regret,

Come on, why

This secret?
